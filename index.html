About

Pronounced Seh-Fee-Eye, this is a realtime music sharing platform, free for anyone to use. Go ahead and browse the different songs (don't worry, they'll auto load for you), or search for something more specific (try something like pop or rock). Every song has been submitted by a user like you, and you too, are free to send in a song of your own! Just click the post button on the lower right and fill out the form! Oh, and in case you were wondering, the login works, but any functionality hasn't been implemented yet, thanks for clicking it though!
