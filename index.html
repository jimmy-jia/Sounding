Welcome to Sounding

How to use Sounding

Click the login button on the top right and login through Facebook.


Facebook login is only used for anti-spam prevention, we will not use your information in any way, nor will we post on Facebook on your behalf.


After logging in, you're free to upload one new post per hour.

For each post, please provide a Youtube link, the song title, the artist, and any description you would like to share! Then watch as your song gets added in realtime to our database, for all users to enjoy!


Thank you for your contribution to a musically diverse world!
